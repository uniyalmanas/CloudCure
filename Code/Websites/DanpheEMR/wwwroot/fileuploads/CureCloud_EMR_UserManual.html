4. AI Clinical SOAP Note Scribe

Advanced AI Feature

The AI Clinical SOAP Scribe is an interactive documentation helper. It utilizes natural language parsing to compile raw, unstructured clinical notes into clinical SOAP blocks inside the CKEditor.

How to Use the Scribe:

1. **Navigate to the FreeText Note Template** in the Clinical module.

2. **Locate the AI Medical Scribe Panel** at the top of the editor.

3. **Type or Paste Raw Unstructured Notes** inside the text area (e.g., *'45 y/o male with sudden onset chest pain since yesterday. BP is 130/85, pulse 80. Clear lungs. Prescribed Aspirin.'*).

4. **Click the 'Generate AI SOAP Note' Button**. The AI engine will parse the text and load color-coded clinical blocks below:

  • Subjective Card: Age, gender, complaints, onset timeline.
  • Objective Card: Vital signs (BP, Pulse) and physical exam findings.
  • Assessment Card: Primary diagnostic impressions.
  • Plan Card: Therapeutic prescriptions and follow-up schedules.

5. **Click 'Apply SOAP to Editor'**. The scribe compiles the reviewed sections into a stunning HTML clinical card and injects it instantly inside your CKEditor workspace.
